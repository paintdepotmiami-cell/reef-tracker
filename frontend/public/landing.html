The Best Free Reef Tank App for Saltwater Aquarium Management

ReefOS is a free reef tank management app designed for saltwater aquarium hobbyists at every level. Whether you're cycling your first tank or maintaining an advanced SPS-dominant mixed reef, ReefOS gives you the tools to track water parameters, manage livestock, monitor equipment, and make data-driven decisions about your reef aquarium.

Unlike basic aquarium tracker apps, ReefOS uses artificial intelligence to read your water test results from photos, identify fish and corals with your camera, generate personalized dosing recommendations, and alert you before small parameter shifts become tank-threatening emergencies. With a database of over 180 reef species, 550+ equipment and supplement products, and 35 specialized reef-keeping tools, ReefOS is the most comprehensive saltwater aquarium app available — and it's completely free.

ReefOS works as a progressive web app (PWA) on any device — iPhone, Android, tablet, or desktop. Your reef data syncs to the cloud in real-time, so you can check your tank status from anywhere. Whether you need a reef parameter tracker, coral compatibility checker, fish feeding schedule, tank cycling guide, or emergency reef troubleshooter, ReefOS has you covered.
